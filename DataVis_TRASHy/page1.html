Comparison of waste management among selected districts

This graph displays multiple parameters on waste management and enables the comparison of selected districts among their profiles. The selection includes, on the one hand, two districts demonstrating high awareness in waste management (Changning, Huangpu) and on the other, two districts which show a high potential of improvement (Pudong, Songjiang). For the general understanding, the bigger the interior surface of each district the higher the potential to improve waste management in the certain district.

Example Pudong (blue line): However Pudong has the second highest generation of waste per person, the waste management infrastructure is poor compared to other districts, as the number of people assigned to a collection or sorting point is very high. In addition, the investment per person in environmental sanitation, assigned for operational costs and long-term investments in waste management, is very low (caution reversed scale!).

Comparing the Pudong with Huangpu for instance, we can see that both produce a high amount of waste per person, but Pudong does not show equivalent action in terms of waste management, hence is a hint for further investigation by the government.
